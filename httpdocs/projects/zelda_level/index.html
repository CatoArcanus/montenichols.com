This project was an early version of level design work for a zelda-style, fire-temple dungeon. First, bubble diagrams were created to represent the space and resource images were gathered. Next a 3d mock-up was created and I speed painted the lava and lighting I was looking for in the scene. The bubble diagrams were then turned into a top-down mock up of the levels to be implemented and then the levels were implemented being modeled in Maya, texture creation in Photoshop, and then animated and lit in UDK. Below are some of these project artifacts: the initial level layout, the level layout with texture considerations, a speed painting establishing lighting. and a final screen shot of the level in action.
